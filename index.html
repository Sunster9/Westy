Westy Dog

Westy Dog is a very annoying dog who always doubles as a mango thief. Very dangerous do not approach, he will steal your mangos, apples, and all other fruits that have been left ungaurded. His most notorious crime was stealing a full bread while mom was fluttering around the kitchen. He got away with nearly 8 breads worth over 5 US dollars.

Biography

Westy was born in the rolling hills of Scotland. So he claims to add mystique to his dog criminal persona, but he was actually born somewhere in Arkansas and grew up stealing mangos, apples, pineapples, and any other foods imagineable. He was taught in the ways of Ninja Food Stealing early in life and has perfected the art of Ninja Food Stealing since that time. He has published numerous academic papers on the art of Ninja Food Stealing and is considered one of the worldwide leaders in the field.
